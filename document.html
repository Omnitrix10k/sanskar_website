Sanskar Admission Documents

Admission Document Checklist

To ensure a smooth and transparent admission process, parents are requested to submit the following documents at the time of admission, as per CBSE and Telangana State guidelines.

Birth Certificate

Issued by Municipal Corporation / Government Authority.

Aadhaar Card (Student)

Mandatory for CBSE registration and official records.

Parent / Guardian Aadhaar

Required for verification and communication purposes.

Transfer Certificate (TC)

Mandatory for admissions from Class II onwards.

Study / Bonafide Certificate

From the previously attended school.

Previous Class Report Card

Academic performance record of the last class attended.

Passport Size Photographs

Recent photographs of the student and parents.

Caste Certificate (If Applicable)

Issued by Telangana State competent authority.

Important Note:
Original documents must be produced for verification at the time of admission. Xerox copies will be retained by the school for official records. Additional documents may be requested as per CBSE or State Government norms.
