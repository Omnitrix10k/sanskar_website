Co-Curricular Activities

Our co-curricular programs nurture creativity, confidence, teamwork, and leadership beyond academics.

  • Sports & Physical Education

    Encouraging physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and a healthy competitive spirit through various sports.

  • Arts & Cultural Activities

    Promoting creativity and expression through music, dance, drawing, drama, and cultural celebrations.

  • Clubs & Skill Development

    Science clubs, literary clubs, and hobby groups that develop critical thinking and communication skills.

  • Leadership & Life Skills

    Building responsibility, confidence, and leadership through student councils, assemblies, and activities.
