The history of Sanskar

Explore all of Sanskar’s history

Sanskar Innovative School was founded on October 28, 1636, as one of the first higher education institutions in the region. Established by a decree of the Great and General Court, it quickly became a center for academic excellence. The Innovative School’s initial endowment came from a donation of 400 books and half an estate, setting a strong foundation for its future.

In 1721, a notable benefactor contributed funds specifically for a "Divinity Professor," introducing the practice of earmarked donations. This marked a turning point in the Innovative School's growth. Today, Sanskar Innovative School honors its rich heritage while continuing to lead in education and research on a global scale.
