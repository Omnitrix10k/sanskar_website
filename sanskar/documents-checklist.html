Documents Checklist

Parents are requested to keep all required documents ready before submitting the admission form. Originals must be carried for verification along with two sets of self-attested photocopies.

  • Keep APAR ID and PEN ID details ready (if issued).
  • Bring both student and parent identity/address proofs.
  • Upload clear scanned copies while applying online.

What Parents Need to Submit

Student Identity & Records

Birth Certificate (Municipal/Authorized copy)
Aadhaar Card copy (student)
APAR ID (if available)
PEN ID (if available)
Previous 2 years report cards / progress sheets
Transfer Certificate (for Class II and above, if applicable)
Bonafide Certificate from previous school (if applicable)
Student passport-size photos (6 copies)

Parent / Guardian Documents

Parent/Guardian Aadhaar copy
PAN Card copy (any one parent/guardian)
Address proof (utility bill/rental agreement/passport)
Parent passport-size photos (2 each)
Valid mobile numbers and email addresses
Emergency contact details (alternate guardian)
Occupation and employer details (optional but recommended)

Additional / Conditional Documents

Medical fitness certificate (recent)
Vaccination / immunization record (if available)
Caste / income certificate (if applicable)
Special needs learning documents (if applicable)
Sibling studying proof at Sanskar (if applicable)
Any court/legal guardianship document (if applicable)

Document requirements may be updated based on CBSE or state notifications. Please contact the admission office for latest clarification before final submission.
