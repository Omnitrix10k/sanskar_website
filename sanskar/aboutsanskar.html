Intro About Sanskar

Shaping Responsible Citizens Through Character and Excellence

Sanskar Innovative School was founded in 2012 by alumni of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya with a clear purpose: to bring the Navodaya standard of education to the present generation. As the first CBSE school in Medchal and one of the respected boarding schools in the state, Sanskar combines strong academics with disciplined value formation.

Set across 4 acres of lush, pollution-free campus, the school carries forward the Gurukul legacy while enabling modern, technology-supported learning spaces. Here, education goes beyond marks and nurtures integrity, ethics, social commitment and national responsibility.
