Two Curriculum Tracks

Dedicated Learning Model for Grades 1-5 and Grades 6-10

Grades 1-5

Primary Foundation

Early years focus on core literacy, numeracy and curiosity-driven learning with strong teacher guidance, activity-based pedagogy and values-led classroom culture.

  • Core areas: English, Mathematics, EVS, 2nd Language, Art & Activity.
  • Weekly structure: Concept blocks + workbook drills + enrichment periods.
  • Skill goals: Reading fluency, number sense, observation and self-expression.
  • Assessments: Monthly review + term-end competency checks.

Grades 6-10

Secondary Advancement

Middle and secondary grades develop higher-order thinking, board readiness and strong subject depth through advanced academic planning, practical exposure and structured exam preparation.

  • Core areas: Science, Mathematics, Social Science, Languages, ICT and Labs.
  • Weekly structure: Subject blocks + practical sessions + revision modules.
  • Skill goals: Analysis, problem solving, writing precision and exam strategy.
  • Assessments: Unit tests, pre-finals and annual exam readiness cycles.

Curriculum Overview

From Foundation to Board Readiness, One Connected Journey

Sanskar's curriculum ensures students build confidence at every stage. Primary grades focus on strong basics and joyful learning, while middle and secondary grades sharpen critical thinking, application and performance readiness for future academic milestones.

  • Balanced academic planning with co-curricular integration.
  • Structured exam preparation without compromising conceptual depth.
  • Regular parent communication on progress, support and interventions.
  • Values-led education aligned to the motto: Moral Values with Modern Education.
