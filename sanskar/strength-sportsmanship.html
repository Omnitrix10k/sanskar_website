NCC - National Cadet Corps

NCC Army Wing Builds Strength, Discipline and National Spirit

Sanskar Innovative School proudly runs NCC (Army Wing) at campus level to develop leadership, physical readiness and patriotism among students. The program reflects our belief that Desha Bhakti (Patriotism) and Daiva Bhakti (Spirituality) must grow together.

  • Affiliated to 1 (T) Arty Bty NCC, Telangana for systematic army-wing training.
  • Structured sessions in fitness, drill and character-building under a full-time qualified ANO.
  • Cadets attend regular camps and receive disaster management plus First Aid skill exposure.
  • Enrollment starts in Class 8 with a two-year progressive training path.
  • Two batches have completed NCC training with cadets showcasing above-par excellence.
Qualified ANO Support Consistent cadet mentoring from a full-time Associate NCC Officer.
Fitness and Readiness Training modules that strengthen stamina, alertness and self-discipline.
