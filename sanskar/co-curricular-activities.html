NCC - National Cadet Corps

NCC Army Wing at Sanskar: Discipline, Leadership and Patriotism in Action

Sanskar Innovative School has another feather in the cap with NCC (Army Wing) at our campus. The program develops discipline, leadership and patriotism, reinforcing our belief that both Desha Bhakti (Patriotism) and Daiva Bhakti (Spirituality) are equally important.

  • Affiliated to 1 (T) Arty Bty NCC, Telangana with a structured training framework.
  • Cadets receive physical fitness, drill and character-building programs under a full-time qualified ANO.
  • Regular camps include disaster management and First Aid skill training for practical readiness.
  • Students enroll from Class 8 and complete a focused two-year cadet preparation journey.
  • Sanskar has successfully completed training for two batches with cadets performing above par.
Full-Time ANO Guidance Dedicated Associate NCC Officer support for continuous cadet mentoring.
Character and Discipline Daily training focused on responsibility, integrity and leadership behavior.
Patriotic Student Culture Strong value alignment with civic duty, national service and social commitment.
