Step-by-Step Admission Process

Follow each stage below to complete your child admission with clarity and confidence.

01
Step 01

Initial Enquiry

Parents connect with our admission team to understand the school philosophy, curriculum, facilities, and grade eligibility.

02
Step 02

Application Submission

The admission form is submitted along with necessary documents to formally register the child for admission.

03
Step 03

Student Interaction

A friendly interaction or age-appropriate assessment helps us understand the child's readiness and learning approach.

04
Step 04

Parent Meeting

Parents meet school leadership to discuss expectations, values, and the child's academic journey.

05
Step 05

Admission Confirmation

Selected students receive admission confirmation along with fee structure and enrollment details.

06
Step 06

Welcome to Sanskar

Orientation, class allocation, and a warm welcome mark the beginning of a meaningful educational journey.
